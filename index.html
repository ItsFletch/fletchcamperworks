About

Made by someone who actually uses this stuff.

Fletch Camper Works makes small, practical camper and motorhome accessories designed around real everyday use. The aim is simple: strong parts, tidy fitting, and products that make life in the van a bit easier.

Each product is made in small batches, checked before posting, and backed by proper support if you have a fitting question.

FAQ

Common questions

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are made and posted within 7–10 days. If demand is high, I’ll let you know.

Will it fit my motorhome?

Many Fiat and Peugeot based cabs share similar layouts, but please message with a photo if you are unsure.

What are the parts made from?

The printed parts are made from PETG, chosen because it is tougher and more heat resistant than standard PLA.

Can I buy two?

Yes. Some products are available as driver and passenger side versions, or as discounted pairs where listed.
